Location

Westgate is conveniently located along Ontdekkers Road, which is the main route between Johannesburg's Northern suburbs and the West Rand suburbs.

And its proximity to the N1 Highway makes it equally accessible to the South and as far as Pretoria.

The centre's navigational coordinates are ( South 26° at 8.3.40 East 27° at 51.828 ).

DIRECTIONS

FROM PRETORIA: Get onto the N1 Highway, travel until 14th Avenue. Turn right at the robot and continue until you reach Ontdekkers Road, turn right onto Ontdekkers. Continue with Ontdekkers until you see Westgate on the left hand side.

FROM PRETORIA ON THE N14 (Pretoria-Krugersdorp Highway): Continue with the N14 (Pretoria-Krugersdorp Highway) until a robot with Keywest shopping centre on the right hand side. Turn left, continue with Voortrekker Road which becomes Ontdekkers Road until you see Westgate on the right hand side.
